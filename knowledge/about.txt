# APOLLO HUB KNOWLEDGE BASE


## COMPANY OVERVIEW
Apollo Hub is a company and community founded by Jacktutorial. While the organization initially explored Roblox-related tools, it has since fully transitioned its focus to website creation, digital design, and creative technology projects - with a commitment to ethical, positive skill-building.


## HISTORICAL BACKGROUND
- Apollo Hub was originally developed as a Roblox serverside executor service
- Due to team resource limitations, efforts to develop or improve exploit-related tools were discontinued, but somewhere in roblox, there is a game that still contained the Serverside fully functional. 
- The previous prototype is still in active use or promoted within the community
- The decision to shift focus was made to create a sustainable, supportive space for building valuable skills that benefit both members and broader communities


## COMMUNITY FEATURES & STRUCTURE
- Learning Channels: Dedicated spaces for discussing web development languages (HTML/CSS/JS), website builders (Carrd, Wix, WordPress, Squarespace), UI/UX design principles, hosting solutions, and domain management
- Project Collaboration Hub: A space where members can post their website or design ideas, find teammates to collaborate with, share work-in-progress builds, and get constructive feedback from the community
- Resource Library: Curated collection of free online courses, design tools (Figma, Canva, Adobe Express), reusable code snippets, and step-by-step guides for creators at all skill levels - from beginners to advanced developers
- Monthly Creative Challenges: Themed projects designed to help members practice their skills (examples include "Build a personal portfolio site in 2 weeks," "Design a landing page for a fictional local business," "Create a responsive blog layout")
- Mentorship Program: A structured initiative where experienced web creators and designers in the community offer one-on-one support, advice, and guidance to those who are just getting started

## COMMUNITY GUIDELINES & CORE VALUES
- All activities and projects must comply with the terms of service and guidelines of any platforms, tools, or services used
- Ethical creation is our priority: We require all members to respect intellectual property rights, avoid harmful practices, and build work that adds value to users and communities
- No discussion, promotion, or sharing of content related to exploiting, hacking, cheating, or any activity that could harm platforms, developers, or other users is permitted in any server channel
- We foster a supportive, inclusive environment where members of all skill levels are welcome to learn, ask questions, and share their work without judgment
