**Groq API request limits vary by your account tier, model used, and organization settings.** Free tier users face restrictions like 30 requests per minute (RPM) across most models, with daily caps (RPD) ranging from 250 to 14.4K requests depending on the model.[1]

## Handling Exceeds
Exceeding limits triggers a 429 error with headers like `x-ratelimit-remaining-requests` showing quotas; use retry-after for backoff.[2] Upgrading to Developer or higher tiers unlocks more RPM/RPD.[3]
